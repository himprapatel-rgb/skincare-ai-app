## Market Gaps & Opportunities

### Underserved Needs

1. **Diverse Skin Tones**
   - Most apps train on limited diversity
   - Opportunity: Train on global dataset
   - Fitzpatrick types 4-6 underrepresented

2. **Male-Specific Concerns**
   - Most apps focus on women
   - Opportunity: Beard/shaving analysis
   - Men's skincare market growing 8% CAGR

3. **Teen Acne Focus**
   - Few apps specialize in teenage skin
   - Opportunity: Age-appropriate recommendations
   - Parent monitoring features

4. **Ingredient Education**
   - Users want to understand ingredients
   - Opportunity: Detailed ingredient analysis
   - "Why this works for YOUR skin" explanations

5. **Community Features**
   - Limited social/community integration
   - Opportunity: Skin twin matching
   - Anonymous routine sharing
